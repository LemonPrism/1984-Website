About This Record

Nineteen Eighty-Four, Re-issued for 2026.

This site is a creative response to George Orwell’s Nineteen Eighty-Four, reimagined as the official online presence of the Ministry of Truth in the present day. Every device we already trust — the phone, the screen, the speaker — takes the role of Orwell’s telescreen. The dystopia is not built from rubble; it is built from good design.

When a system erases truth, language, and individuality, fear becomes the architecture of control — but the act of thinking freely, loving, or simply feeling, is itself a form of rebellion, even if it is ultimately crushed.
