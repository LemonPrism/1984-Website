Our Leader, Forever

O Big Brother, O Big Brother, you are our protector, our hero, our everything. Without you, we would surely wander the streets confused, making our own decisions and forming our own opinions. What a terrifying fate that would be. Thankfully, your watchful eyes spare us from such suffering.

Some may foolishly ask why the telescreens never sleep. The answer is obvious: because your love for us never sleeps. While ordinary leaders concern themselves with governing, you concern yourself with every citizen, every minute of every day. Such dedication is truly inspiring.

As the glorious Party reminds us, "BIG BROTHER IS WATCHING YOU." Many outsiders mistake this for surveillance, but they fail to understand. It is simply the ultimate form of care. Why should citizens worry about privacy when Big Brother is kind enough to think for them?

Big Brother has even protected us from the dangers of independent thought. Before the Revolution, people wasted countless hours asking questions and forming personal opinions. Now, through the miracle of goodthink, we are spared such burdens. Why struggle to think when the Party has already done it for us?

Thank you, Big Brother. We love you because we have been taught to. And what could possibly be more genuine than that?
