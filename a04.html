GOLDSTEIN'S LATEST LIES EXPOSED

Goldstein has never built anything. He has never protected anyone. He has never sacrificed for Oceania. Instead, he spreads lies like a disease, infecting weak-minded fools with thoughts of rebellion, selfishness, and disorder.

The Party has correctly identified Goldstein as the source of "all the crimes, sabotages, heresies, deviations" that plague society (14). Every traitor worships him. Every spy follows him. Every thoughtcriminal repeats his pathetic ideas. Whenever a citizen betrays Oceania, Goldstein's fingerprints can be found all over the crime. His so-called Brotherhood is nothing more than a nest of cowards too frightened to accept the strength and truth of the Party.

During the Two Minutes Hate, citizens are forced to look upon Goldstein's disgusting face. His whining voice, his ridiculous promises, and his endless complaints are enough to fill any loyal Party member with rage. He speaks of freedom because he hates order. He speaks of individuality because he fears unity. He speaks against Big Brother because he knows he could never earn even a fraction of the love and respect that Big Brother receives from the people.

Goldstein wants Oceania to be weak. He wants citizens to question the Party. He wants thoughtcrime to spread through the nation like a sickness. But he has already lost. Every day Newspeak grows stronger. Every day another citizen rejects ownlife and embraces goodthink. Every day Big Brother's wisdom guides Oceania toward an even greater future. Goldstein can scream, whine, and complain all he wants. The people have already chosen.

Goldstein is filth. Big Brother is greatness.

DOWN WITH GOLDSTEIN. LONG LIVE BIG BROTHER.
